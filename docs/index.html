About FortiPath

FortiPath is a cutting-edge tool tailored for Executive Protection (EP) and Advance agents. In the ever-evolving landscape of security threats, FortiPath stands out by emphasizing the importance of "hunting the hunter." Our platform is designed to empower agents with proactive cyber reconnaissance, ensuring that threats are detected and neutralized long before they pose a risk to the principal. With a focus on threat hunting, FortiPath transforms the traditional reactive security approach into a proactive stance, significantly enhancing the effectiveness of protection teams and ensuring the safety of the principal.

Key Features

  • Threat Hunting: Actively search for signs of planned attacks or reconnaissance against the principal in online forums, dark web, and other platforms.
  • Penetration Testing: Test the cybersecurity defenses of locations the principal will visit, identifying vulnerabilities.
  • Red Teaming: Simulate real-world attacks on the principal's digital assets to test the effectiveness of current security measures.
  • Digital Countermeasures: Deploy systems like digital decoys and active cyber defense to counter cyber threats.
  • Information Warfare: Utilize disinformation campaigns and social media manipulation to deter or mislead potential threats.
  • Collaboration with Law Enforcement: Enhance security through joint operations and intelligence sharing with local agencies.
  • Technological Advancements: Leverage AI-powered threat detection and drone surveillance for faster and more accurate threat detection.
